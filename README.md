# Commercial Product Design: Group Mortality Projection for Financial Risk Solutions

ACST3059 Actuarial Modelling Group Case Study (2023)

Group project focused on Australian mortality projection using statistical learning for declining trends. Designed commercial financial/insurance products (e.g., annuities, longevity bonds) to address longevity risk, with pricing and financial impact analysis.

## Key Tasks
- Collaboratively projected mortality trends  
- Applied statistical models for longevity risk assessment  
- Developed commercial product pitch with pricing and risk implications  

## Skills Demonstrated
- Group collaboration on mortality projection  
- Statistical learning for longevity risk modelling  
- Commercial product design, pricing, and financial solution pitch
